### /analyze

**Purpose:** To perform a deep analysis of the current system, codebase, or specific files to understand dependencies, identify issues, and assess potential impacts.

**Usage:** `/analyze "[Description of what to analyze, e.g., 'the user authentication module', 'the database schema', 'the codebase for potential refactoring']"`

**Description:**
The Planner Agent uses this command to gain a comprehensive understanding of the project's state. It will identify how proposed changes might affect other parts of the system and help in formulating a robust risk mitigation strategy.
